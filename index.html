Meet Jasmine

Your hair artist & beauty enthusiast

Hey beautiful! I'm Jasmine, a 20-year-old entrepreneur with a passion for making women feel absolutely gorgeous. My journey started when I was just 15, practicing on family and friends, and now I'm living my dream of running my own salon!

I specialize in protective styles, natural hair care, and creating looks that make you feel confident and radiant. Every head of hair tells a story, and I'm here to help you write yours with style, grace, and a whole lot of glamour.

When I'm not behind the chair, you'll find me researching the latest trends, perfecting new techniques, or planning my next business venture. I believe that beauty is powerful, and every woman deserves to feel like the queen she is!
